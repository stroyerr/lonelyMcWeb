Features




Towny

Lose yourself in the action of LonelyMC towny!

LonelyMC has a variety of both popular and custom made plugins that work coherently to bring the ultimate towny expirience to LonelyMC.

In game loterries, Jobs, Towns, Resource Worlds, Wild plugins, server map and more are just some of the ways LonelyMC brings your expirience to life.




Events

Find yourself lost in the fun and rewarding events we host.

LonelyMC has it's very own, custom coded plugins for server-wide events.

Find yourself diving into our Lucky Block events, constantly winning
free money, items and commands.

Or perhaps you want to be able to compete with other players in a sudo-parkour PvP
voting event?
No problem! Every time the server reaches 10 votes within 24 hours,
a voters event automatically starts, and all voters within the
last 24 hours are automatically invited!




Ranks

Lose yourself in the progression of LonelyMC ranks!

LonelyMC has a variety of ranks for those who love to grind and also for those looking to support the server.

Grind some money and xp in game, giving yourself the freedom to /rankup at
any time, or choose to support the server with /buy and get some
awesome events, commands and access for your loyalty!




Slimefun

From backpacks to jetpacks, the possibilities are limitless!

Grind enough and you can have a jetpack, autominer or even your own custom items.
